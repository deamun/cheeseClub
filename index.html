Carlton Cheese Club

If you enjoy good cheeses and would love to learn more about them, join our cheese club for a carefully curated selection of cheeses delivered to your door every month!

The subscription provides three cheeses, specially selected to showcase the best cheeses in season this month.

Every cheese is accompanied by an information card detailing the cheese's origin and history, parents (the producer), tasting notes and flavour profile, optimal pairings, and even cooking suggestions!

This Month's Cheeses

Burrata is an incredibly soft stretched curd cheese, originating from the Puglia region of South East Italy, first created in approximately 1920. Handmade daily in Carlton, Melbourne using only fresh Australian cows milk, the supple outer mozzarella shell contains a luxuriously soft and delicate interior made of shredded mozzarella and cream.

If you can resist devouring it as it is, try spreading on warm crusty sourdough, with fresh cracked pepper!

La Primavera pays homage to the moment spring emerges in Adelaide Hills. Made from the first raw milk of the spring season, using classic alpine techniques to create a truly unique cheese. The aroma of dried spring flowers contrast the slightly pungent rind, and the paste has a rich buttery flavour balanced by a delicate nutiness - think butterscotch flavours!

Section28 only makes La Primavera with spring milk and is available from February to April each year.

A true Fermier Cheese, made by hand from milk produced by animals raised on the cheesemaker's property, right here in Victoria! Dreaming Goat Dairy's Chevre is a reflection of exceptional farming practices and cheesemaking. It is fresh, with hints of lemon curd, extremely clean flavour, with a rich tongue coating mouthfeel. Tastes of fresh clean goats milk with a hint of lactic fermentation.

A stunning example of quality through simple perfection.

Our Local Producers and Partners

Founded by third generation French cheesemaker Matthieu Megard, L'Artisan cheese highlights the terroir of the Great Ocean Road. Using only organic milk sourced predominantly from the Smith Family Organic Farm on the Great Ocean Road, they use the best Australia has to offer following recipes and techniques brought from France.

Located in Carlton, the Italian heart of Melbourne, this small mozzarella lab uses fresh Australian milk and authentic Italian traditions daily to hand stretch and shape extraordinary orbs of mozzarella, as well as yoghurt and fresh milk refills.

Section28 handcrafts alpine style cheese of the highest quality and each cheese is made using traditional methods and aged to create products that are inspired by classic European cheeses. Their cheeses remain uniquely Australian in style and flavour, highlighting the quality of the raw milk, and quintessentially capturing the terroir of the Adelaide Hills.

Milk The Cow is a licensed fromagerie; a late night cheese bar serving over 180 different cheeses from all around the world, expertly curated and cared for by their team of cheesemongers. Their signature menu item is no doubt the flights: cheeseboards with a selection of cheeses perfectly matched with accompanying boutique beverages.

When Sarah Ajzner started Dreaming Goat Dairy, they had a herd of 24 milking goats and a vision. Since then, their herd has continued to grow, and the quality of their cheese has improved continuously. They believe that it is an incredible privelege to be entrusted with, and trusted by their herd - and it shows in the quality of their products, with their chevre winning the Gold Medal at the Victorian Produce awards.
